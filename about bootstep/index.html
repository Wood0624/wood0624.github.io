Top 5 best cars in movies and tv shows

#1: The batmobile

from the movie "Batman & Batman Returns" starring Michael Keaton and directed by Tim Burton. The batmobile contains an arsenal of gadgets including grappling hooks, machine guns, and the ability to turn into a missile to pass through narrow spaces.

#2: Mach-5

From the 1960's cartoon and the live-action movie "Speed Racer", the Mach-5 is driven by the main character speed racer. Like the batmobile, the Mach-5 has many arsenals while on the race track. Such as Belt tires while driving on unsteady ground, Cutter blades to get through heavily wooded terrain, and a bulletproof shield glass.

#3: Fab-1

From the British puppet show "Thunderbirds" created by Gerry Anderson, and modeled after Rolls-Royce. Fab-1 is driven by Lady Penelope and her butler Parker, this car contains a guns hidden inside the front and back of the car. As well as the ability to drive on water, and fly into space.

#4:Bumblebee

From the movie "Transformers" based on the 2007 Chevrolet Camaro, Bumblebee is the only car on this list that can transform into a giant robot to fight against the Decepticons. Bumblebee disguises himself as a Camaro in blend in on earth.

#5:Delorean

From the movie "Back to the Future". One of the most famous and popular cars in pop culture. The DeLorean can travel forwards and backward through time, and can even fly.
