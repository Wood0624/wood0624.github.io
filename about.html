What to expect during construction.

With construction starting in April 2019 we anticipate the new arrival of the ARC building as early as April 2021.

The current SA Fitness Zone and gym will be operating and open as usual. Please visit the Fitness Zone and the Gymnasium for more on the hours, offerings, and pricing.

About

Who

is ARC? We are the Athletics and Recreation Centre. This is an exciting venture for Algonquin Community.This facility will provide our students with a place to break through perceptual barries in welcoming, safe, inclusive and inviting environment for students of all background, cultures and abilities to spark a movement.

What

is it going to cost me? This facility will be included in your activity fee. All fee-paying students can use and participate in all activites in the building. As for the facility. It will cost Algonquin College $48.8 million.

Where

will the ARC buidling be? The ARC building will be located northeast of the Student Commons building, oppostie of the residence. The facility will be estimated 100,000 square feet! Connected to the Students Commons by a "Plus 15 Pedestrian Link.
